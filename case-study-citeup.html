CITEUP Event Branding

Visual identity for "Node of Connection". Applied across digital and print assets.

01

The Problem

Large-scale tech university events often suffer from generic "cyber" branding that fails to differentiate them or build a sense of community. The challenge was to create a comprehensive visual identity for CITEUP 2025 that embodied the concept of networking and synergy, while maintaining enough flexibility to be applied across diverse touchpoints: from mobile apps to 10-meter physical banners.

02

Research & Insights

Through workshops with the event organizers and surveys of past attendees, we formulated the "Node of Connection" philosophy.

Insight 1: Fragmentation

Past events had different visual styles for their web presence vs. their physical merchandise, leading to brand dilution.

Insight 2: "Instagrammability"

Gen Z attendees strongly prefer bold, typography-heavy designs for physical photo-ops over traditional corporate logos.
