    N --> O[Toast: 'Saved to your Wishlist ✓'\nNo account? → Soft prompt to sign up\nGuest can still bookmark with local storage]
    O --> P{User logged in?}
    P -- No → nudge --> Q[Non-intrusive banner:\n'Sign up to keep your saves forever'\nDismissable — not blocking]
    P -- Yes --> R

    M -- 📍 Check in --> S{User logged in?}
    S -- No --> T[Auth wall with context:\n'Log in to check in at this spot'\nSign up or continue as guest info]
    T --> U[/Auth Flow/\nEmail / Google / Phone OTP\nFast — under 3 screens]
    U --> S
    S -- Yes --> V[Tap 'Check In' button\nConfirmation sheet slides up:\nVenue name · timestamp · optional photo]
    V --> W[Check-in confirmed ✅\nBadge added to user profile\nVenue's check-in count increments\nSocial feed can show activity]

    W --> R
    M -- ✍️ Leave a review --> X{User checked in\nor visited?}
    X -- Yes --> Y[/Review Composer/ <br> Star rating (1–5)\nText field: 'What made this spot special?'\nPhoto upload optional]
    X -- No → prompt --> Z[Soft gate: 'Have you visited this place?'\nTwo options: 'I've been here' or\n'Just sharing thoughts?'\nBoth paths allowed — honesty label]
    Z --> Y
    Y --> AA[Submit review\nModeration flag check\nReview appears after brief delay]
    AA --> AB([✅ Review live · Profile updated\nXP / badge earned if gamification enabled])

    R --> F
